Quarterly Grade Break Down

Students in grades 9 - 12, per MSCS standards, are required to have a minimum of 17 grades per quarter, broken down as follows:

Type Min. Amount Grade Weight (%)
Assessments 4 40%
Classwork 4 35%
Homework 4 10%
Projects 1 10%
Class Participation 4 5%

  • Standard: N/A
  • Objective: N/A
  • Agenda:
    1. Create a visual representation of your daily schedule. (Classwork)
      • The easiest way to do this is to use the method of creating calendar events in your phone.
      • An additional method, albeit with some drawbacks, is to create a daily or weekly schedule on a sheet of paper. The main drawback to this is that it doesn't allow for reminders or notifications.
    2. Get laptop assignment. (Class Participation)
      • This will be a guided activity and will require your attention.
      • Anyone who cannot comply with the procedure for the laptops will have to figure their own way to do the classwork.
    3. Confirm access to Teams. (Class Participation)
      • This will be required for bellwork, closure, and discussion questions going forward.
    4. Confirm access to PowerSchool. (Class Participation)
