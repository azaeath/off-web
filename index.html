Rush Jobs

  • Rush jobs are available unless otherwise stated.
  • Rush jobs are 'Total Cost of Request x 1.5'
    • For further clarification: Total Cost + 50%
    • For even further clarification: If your piece is $60, 50% of this is $30, so your total would be $90. ($60 x 1.5 = $90)
  • Rush jobs are done as soon as possible with the exception of days reserved for rest.
  • If people have ordered rush jobs ahead of you, I will give you an estimate of when yours will be likely to start.

Illustrations

Currently, I'm primarily doing character illustrations until I create visual references for illustrations that include background work. However, I am offering illustrations with BG work included, regardless.
  • Extras Tax: An additional fee tacked on to anything that would take a lot of extra time like complicated props, intricate character designs, magical effects, etc. This tax can be situational, for instance:
    • I won't charge for simple additions like a handbag or a hat, but an elaborate weapon that might take more time than the character would incur this tax.
    • I can easily sketch things by implying that they are there, but if it needs to go beyond a sketch, it might incur the tax.
    • I won't tax animal ears, tails, horns, and the like, but if a character is half animal (assuming I accept your request of this type), it would incur the tax.
    • I will only tax you if I'm sure it will take extra time, but I will always let you know beforehand.
  • Prices are per character. You can ask for additional characters in a piece, but bear in mind that it will cost more because drawing multiple characters in a scene, especially if they are in perspective, is an even more difficult process than just drawing one.
