Who is he?

An artist from Memphis, TN, Justin, better known as Azaeath online, considers himself a "jack of all trades, master of learning." With his affinity for learning new things, he has become proficient in different forms of art and prefers to dip his hand into any artistic project related to video games and those who create/utilize them for entertainment whether it be professionally or for hobby.
